Help using this page
You can use your apprenticeship earnings summary to:
  • look back at the running total earnings, planned ESFA payments and co-investment contributions that were calculated for each month of the funding year to date, based on the learner data you submitted
  • identify the specific learners that affected your expected payment amounts or needed co-investment money collecting for them
For help understanding payments and earnings read our full guidance on understanding your apprenticeship earnings summary.
